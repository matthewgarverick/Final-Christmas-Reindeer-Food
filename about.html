Nicole Antoinette

I wrote Christmas Reindeer Food on December 25, 2005. Ten years later,
she released the book on January 1, 2016. Then on October 15, 2018, she
released the sequel Christmas Reindeer Food Mishap.

Antoinette Consulting d.b.a. Nicole Antoinette Publishing and Faith Books &
MORE Publishing. Book buyers know her for leading efforts to publish work
as "A Catalyst for Change.""
